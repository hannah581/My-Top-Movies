Divergent

Divergent is a dystopian science fiction film based on the novel by Veronica Roth.

It is set in a future society divided into five factions, each representing a different virtue: Abnegation (selflessness), Amity (peace), Candor (honesty), Dauntless (bravery), and Erudite (knowledge).

The story follows Tris Prior, a young woman who discovers she is "Divergent" and does not fit into any one faction. As she uncovers a conspiracy to destroy all Divergents, Tris must learn to embrace her identity and fight for her survival.

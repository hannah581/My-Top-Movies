Lady Bird

Back to Homepage

Lady Bird is a coming-of-age film directed by Greta Gerwig, released in 2017. It follows the story of a high school senior, Christine "Lady Bird" McPherson, as she navigates her relationships with her mother, friends, and romantic interests.

The film explores themes of identity, family dynamics, and the challenges of adolescence.

With its witty dialogue and strong performances, "Lady Bird" received critical acclaim and was nominated for several Academy Awards.

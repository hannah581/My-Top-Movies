Five Feet Apart

Back to Homepage

Five Feet Apart is a romantic drama film that tells the story of two teenagers with cystic fibrosis who fall in love but must maintain a safe distance from each other to avoid cross-infection.

Directed by Justin Baldoni, the film stars Cole Sprouse and Haley Lu Richardson.

Released in 2019, it explores themes of love, illness, and the challenges of living with a chronic condition.
